# SMS KPI Tree

Version: 0.1

## North Star Metric

Enterprise Sustainability Revenue Index:

```text
(Qualified Pipeline x Funnel Velocity x Win Probability) / Customer Acquisition Cost
```

## KPI Tree

```text
Enterprise Sustainability Revenue Index
  |- Qualified Pipeline
  |   |- Target accounts identified
  |   |- Engaged accounts
  |   |- MQLs generated
  |   |- SQLs accepted
  |   |- Opportunities created
  |   |- Pipeline coverage
  |
  |- Funnel Velocity
  |   |- Lead to MQL days
  |   |- MQL to SQL days
  |   |- SQL to Opportunity days
  |   |- Opportunity to Closure days
  |   |- Proposal turnaround time
  |
  |- Win Probability
  |   |- MQL to SQL conversion
  |   |- SQL to Opportunity conversion
  |   |- Opportunity to Win Rate
  |   |- Buying committee completeness
  |   |- Proof asset coverage
  |
  |- Customer Acquisition Cost
      |- Campaign spend
      |- Cost per engaged account
      |- Cost per MQL
      |- Cost per SQL
      |- CAC payback period
```

## System-Level KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Cadence | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Enterprise Sustainability Revenue Index | Qualified pipeline multiplied by funnel velocity and win probability, divided by CAC | Monthly | Director Marketing + Sales Head |
| Qualified Pipeline | Pipeline from ICP-fit accounts with validated need and buying intent | Weekly | Sales Head |
| Funnel Velocity | Speed of movement from lead to closure | Biweekly | Revenue Operations Lead |
| Win Probability | Conversion strength across SQL, opportunity, proposal, and win stages | Weekly | Sales Head |
| CAC | Total acquisition cost for a solution or campaign cohort | Monthly | Marketing Head + Finance |
| Revenue Sourced | Revenue directly created by marketing-originated demand | Monthly | Marketing Head |
| Revenue Influenced | Revenue supported by marketing activity or proof assets | Monthly | Marketing Head |

| WF7 BOFU Conversion Enablement | Proposal to win conversion | Proposal turnaround, ROI analysis completion, procurement cycle time |
| WF8 Funnel Velocity Optimization | Stage delay reduction | Lead to MQL days, MQL to SQL days, opportunity aging |
| WF9 MQL to SQL to Closure Engine | Opportunity to Win Rate | MQLs, SQLs, pipeline coverage, handoff SLA |
| WF10 ROI And Proof Loop | Revenue influenced by proof | Campaign ROI, case studies completed, reference accounts |

## Review Questions

Weekly:

- Are leads coming from ICP-fit accounts?
- Are MQLs accepted by Sales?
- Which buying committee roles are missing?
- Which opportunities need proof, ROI, or executive support?
- Which funnel stage is slowing revenue movement?

Monthly:

- Which solution has the strongest pipeline economics?
- Which sector has the strongest response?
- Which proof assets are missing?
- What should be changed in ICP, messaging, or offer?
- Which campaigns should receive more or less investment?
