# SMS Governance Cadence

Version: 0.1

## Weekly SMS Revenue Standup

Owner: Revenue Operations Lead

Participants:

- Marketing
- Sales
- Solution Consultants
- Revenue Operations
- Sustainability/ESG SMEs when domain or proof questions are active

Agenda:

1. Review target account engagement.
2. Review MQLs, SQLs, and opportunity creation.
3. Review handoff SLA and rejection reasons.
4. Review active opportunities and buying committee gaps.
5. Review proposal, ROI, and proof blockers.
6. Assign next actions by owner.

Output:

- Updated weekly SMS dashboard
- Bottleneck log
- Handoff and rejection reason log
- Next action list

## Biweekly Funnel Velocity Review

Owner: Revenue Operations Lead

Purpose:

Reduce stage delays across Lead to MQL, MQL to SQL, SQL to Opportunity, and Opportunity to Closure.

Agenda:

1. Review stage duration.
2. Identify slowest stage.
3. Review root causes.
4. Decide experiment or workflow change.
5. Assign owner and target metric.

Output:

- Funnel velocity report
- Experiment brief
- Updated SLA or workflow

## Monthly SMS System Health Review

Owner: Director Marketing

Participants:

- Marketing Head
- Sales Head
- Revenue Operations Lead
- Solution Consultants
- Sustainability/ESG SMEs
- Finance
- Customer Success where proof assets are reviewed

Agenda:

1. Review Enterprise Sustainability Revenue Index.
2. Review solution-level pipeline.
3. Review ICP and target account fit.
4. Review MQL to SQL to closure performance.
5. Review ROI and proof asset creation.
6. Decide solution focus and budget shifts.
7. Approve architecture changes.

Output:

- System health score
- Solution priority decisions
- Architecture changes
- Capability-building actions

## Monthly Proof And ROI Review

Owner: Marketing Head

Purpose:

Turn delivered outcomes into revenue proof.

Agenda:

1. Review closed-won and delivered accounts.
2. Review sustainability outcomes.
3. Review reference candidates.
4. Decide case studies, testimonials, ROI proof, and sales enablement updates.

Output:

- Proof asset plan
- Case study pipeline
- Reference account list
- Updated campaign and sales assets
