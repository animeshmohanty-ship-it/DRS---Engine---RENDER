# DMS KPI Tree

Version: 0.1

## North Star Metric

DRS Adoption Index:

```text
(Public Awareness x Stakeholder Alignment x Return Participation) / Trust And Friction Barriers
```

## KPI Tree

```text
DRS Adoption Index
  |- Public Awareness
  |   |- Awareness reach
  |   |- Message comprehension
  |   |- Retail asset deployment
  |   |- School/community sessions
  |   |- Social engagement
  |   |- Search or inquiry volume
  |
  |- Stakeholder Alignment
  |   |- Priority stakeholders mapped
  |   |- Priority stakeholders briefed
  |   |- Champion commitments
  |   |- Open concerns resolved
  |   |- Retailer participation readiness
  |
  |- Return Participation
  |   |- Containers returned
  |   |- Return rate
  |   |- Active users
  |   |- Repeat returns
  |   |- Retail participation
  |
  |- Trust And Friction Barriers
      |- Negative sentiment
      |- Misinformation incidents
      |- Unresolved stakeholder concerns
      |- Machine or return point friction
      |- Cost per container
      |- Retailer complaints
```

## System-Level KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Cadence | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| DRS Adoption Index | Public awareness multiplied by stakeholder alignment and return participation, divided by trust/friction barriers | Weekly during launch, monthly after stabilization | DMS Pod Lead |
| Public Awareness | Reach and comprehension of DRS message in the target geography | Weekly | Marketing Head |
| Stakeholder Readiness Score | Priority stakeholders mapped, briefed, and aligned | Weekly before launch | Government Affairs Head |
| Launch Readiness Score | Completion of T-minus milestones across awareness, retail, media, machine/demo, and event readiness | Weekly or twice weekly near launch | DMS Pod Lead |
| Return Rate | Containers returned divided by estimated eligible containers | Weekly after launch | Operations Head |
| Cost Per Container | Total collection/activation cost divided by containers returned | Weekly after launch | Operations Head |
| Sentiment Trend | Direction of public and media sentiment | Weekly during launch | Communications Head |

## Review Questions

Weekly before launch:

- Which stakeholder can still block or weaken launch?
- Which public question is not answered clearly?
- Are communication promises aligned with operational readiness?
- Which T-minus milestone is most at risk?
- What must be resolved before consumer awareness scales?

Weekly after launch:

- Are people returning containers as expected?
- Which locations underperform and why?
- Which trust or friction barrier is hurting participation?
- What story can we amplify with evidence?
- Which learning should update the next geography playbook?
