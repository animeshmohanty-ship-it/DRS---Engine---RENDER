Stage 17 · Governance & Cadence ★ rituals engine

The cadence that keeps communication and operations in sync

DailyT-7 → T+14 launch-window check-in: issues, sentiment, escalations, rapid-response approvals.
WeeklyDMS workflow standup: readiness, narrative gaps, biggest friction barrier, next actions → weekly dashboard.
BiweeklyWorkflow improvement review: fix the one workflow dragging adoption.
MonthlySystem-health review: Adoption Index trend, architecture changes, geography priority.
QuarterlyGeography performance review + playbook update for the next launch.
Escalation rules route to Director Marketing / DMS Pod Lead / Comms Head / Ops Head depending on the trigger.
