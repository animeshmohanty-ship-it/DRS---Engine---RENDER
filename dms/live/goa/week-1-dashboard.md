## Learning This Week

Stakeholder learning: Goa requires a stakeholder-first approach; government, pollution board, tourism, municipal, retailer, and brand alignment must precede public awareness.

Consumer learning: Primary behavior barrier is expected to be process clarity: what to return, where to return, and how the deposit/reward works.

Retail learning: Retail readiness is likely the operational hinge; store staff must understand their role before consumer demand is created.

Operational learning: DMS cannot scale communication until machine count, return points, logistics, and issue escalation are confirmed.

Reputation learning: The biggest reputation risk is public awareness moving faster than operational readiness.
