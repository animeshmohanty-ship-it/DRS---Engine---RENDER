# DMS Governance Cadence

Version: 0.1

## Weekly DMS Workflow Standup

Owner: DMS Pod Lead

Participants:

- Marketing
- Government Affairs
- Communications
- Operations
- Retail/Partner Teams
- Data Team when dashboards are active

Agenda:

1. Review geography launch phase.
2. Review stakeholder readiness.
3. Review narrative and FAQ gaps.
4. Review consumer awareness readiness.
5. Review launch readiness or collection performance.
6. Identify the biggest trust or friction barrier.
7. Assign next actions by owner.

Output:

- Updated weekly DMS dashboard
- Concern and friction log
- Next action list

## Biweekly Workflow Improvement Review

Owner: Functional Head based on bottleneck

Purpose:

Improve the workflow causing the biggest drag on DRS adoption.

Agenda:

1. Select one workflow bottleneck.
2. Review evidence from stakeholder, awareness, reputation, or collection data.
3. Identify root cause.
4. Change workflow, template, message, SLA, or owner behavior.
5. Decide next two-week experiment.

Output:

- Workflow improvement decision
- Updated workflow SOP or template
- Experiment owner and target metric

## Monthly DMS System Health Review

Owner: Director Marketing

Participants:

- DMS Pod Lead
- Marketing Head
- Government Affairs Head
- Communications Head
- Operations Head
- Retail/Partner Teams
- Data Team

Agenda:

1. Review DRS Adoption Index trend.
2. Review stakeholder alignment.
3. Review public awareness and message comprehension.
4. Review launch readiness or collection performance.
5. Review reputation and sentiment.
6. Decide geography focus or next launch readiness.
7. Approve architecture changes.

Output:

- System health score
- Architecture changes
- Geography priority decisions
- Capability-building actions

## Daily Launch Window Check-In

Owner: DMS Pod Lead

Timing:

- Recommended from T-7 to T+14.

Agenda:

1. Review operational issues.
2. Review media and public sentiment.
3. Review stakeholder escalations.
4. Review retail or return point friction.
5. Approve rapid response actions.

Output:

- Daily issue log
- Rapid response actions
- Operational handoffs
