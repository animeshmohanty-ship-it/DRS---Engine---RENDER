# SMS Workflow Library

Version: 0.1

## Workflow Map

| ID | Workflow | Primary Owner | Primary Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| WF1 | Domain Intelligence | Marketing | Credible sector and solution expertise |
| WF2 | Target Account Selection | Marketing | Prioritized high-fit account universe |
| WF3 | Buying Committee Mapping | Sales | Multi-stakeholder account understanding |
| WF4 | Persona-Led Communication | Marketing | Messages and assets by role |
| WF5 | TOFU Demand Creation | Marketing | Awareness and engaged demand |
| WF6 | MOFU Evaluation Activation | Marketing | Assessments, workshops, and consultations |
| WF7 | BOFU Conversion Enablement | Sales Enablement | Proposal, ROI, and procurement support |
| WF8 | Funnel Velocity Optimization | Revenue Operations | Reduced stage delays |
| WF9 | MQL to SQL to Closure Engine | Sales + Marketing | Disciplined handoff and conversion |
| WF10 | ROI And Proof Loop | Marketing | Case studies, references, and ROI proof |

## Workflow Sequence

```text
WF1 Domain Intelligence
  -> WF2 Target Account Selection
  -> WF3 Buying Committee Mapping
  -> WF4 Persona-Led Communication
  -> WF5 TOFU Demand Creation
  -> WF6 MOFU Evaluation Activation
  -> WF7 BOFU Conversion Enablement
  -> WF8 Funnel Velocity Optimization
  -> WF9 MQL to SQL to Closure Engine
  -> WF10 ROI And Proof Loop
  -> WF1 Domain Intelligence
```

## WF1: Domain Intelligence

Purpose:

Build the subject matter expertise required to sell enterprise sustainability solutions credibly.

Inputs:

- Regulations
- Competitor analysis
- Industry pain points
- Customer conversations
- Use cases
- Delivery data
- Analyst or market reports

Activities:

- Study regulations.
- Analyze competitors.
- Identify sector pain points.
- Build use cases.
- Create industry playbooks.
- Produce benchmark reports.

Outputs:

- Domain playbook
- Sector-specific messaging
- Benchmark report
- Use-case library
- Regulation brief

KPIs:

- Domain playbooks completed
- Benchmark reports completed
- Sales questions answered by playbook
- Content assets sourced from domain insights
- SME validation score

RACI:

- Responsible: Marketing
- Accountable: Marketing Head
- Consulted: Sustainability/ESG SMEs, Sales, Solution Consultants
- Informed: Director Marketing

AI agents used:

- Regulation summarization agent
- Competitor research agent
- Playbook drafting agent

Templates:

- Domain playbook
- Regulation brief
- Benchmark report

Review frequency:

- Monthly

Optimization triggers:

- Sales cannot answer domain questions.
- Regulation changes.
- Competitor positioning shifts.
- Campaign messages feel generic.

## WF2: Target Account Selection

Purpose:

Identify companies most likely to need, value, and buy the solution.

Inputs:

- Domain playbook
- Regulatory obligation signals
- Sector list
- Company size
- Sustainability maturity
- Installed base or relationship data
- TAM/SAM/SOM assumptions

Activities:

- Define ICP.
- Segment accounts into Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3.
- Estimate TAM/SAM/SOM.
- Build target account list.
- Score accounts by obligation, urgency, fit, and access.

Outputs:

- ICP definition
- Target account list
- TAM/SAM/SOM estimate
- Account scoring model
- Priority account tiers

KPIs:

- ICP completion
- Target accounts identified
- Tier 1 accounts with complete data
- Account fit score
- Sales acceptance of target list

RACI:

- Responsible: Marketing
- Accountable: Marketing Head
- Consulted: Sales, Revenue Operations, Sustainability/ESG SMEs
- Informed: Director Marketing, Sales Head

AI agents used:

- Account research agent
- TAM/SAM/SOM assistant
- ICP scoring assistant

Templates:

- ICP definition
- Target account list
- Account scoring model

Review frequency:

- Monthly

Optimization triggers:

- Sales rejects accounts as low fit.
- Campaign response is weak.
- New regulation changes account priority.

## WF3: Buying Committee Mapping

Purpose:

Identify decision makers and influencers inside target accounts so opportunities do not become single-threaded.

Inputs:

- Target account list
- Existing CRM data
- Sales notes
- LinkedIn or public role data
- Solution buying process

Activities:

- Map Sustainability Head, Procurement Head, Compliance Manager, CSR Head, ESG Team, Plant Head, CFO, Government Affairs Head, Packaging Head, and Retail Operations Head where relevant.
- Identify economic buyer, technical buyer, champion, blocker, and procurement owner.
- Capture persona pain and influence.
- Update account intelligence sheet.

Outputs:

- Buying committee map
- Account intelligence sheet
- Champion/blocker view
- Persona engagement plan

KPIs:

- Target accounts with mapped buying committee
- Opportunities with multi-threaded contacts
- Champion identified rate
- Blocker identified rate
- Account intelligence completeness

RACI:

- Responsible: Sales
- Accountable: Sales Head
- Consulted: Marketing, Revenue Operations, Solution Consultants
- Informed: Director Marketing

AI agents used:

- Buying committee research assistant
- Account intelligence summarizer
- Persona gap detector

Templates:

- Buying committee map
- Account intelligence sheet
- Persona engagement plan

Review frequency:

- Weekly for active opportunities
- Monthly for target account universe

Optimization triggers:

- Opportunities stall with one contact.
- Procurement appears late.
- CFO or Compliance concerns are not addressed.

## WF4: Persona-Led Communication

Purpose:

Create messaging and assets that speak to each buying committee member's priorities.

Inputs:

- Domain playbook
- Buying committee map
- Persona pain points
- Sales objections
- Proof assets
- Solution value proposition

Activities:

- Build persona messaging matrix.
- Create email, landing page, deck, and webinar messaging.
- Write objection responses.
- Define proof points by persona.
- Update sales enablement.

Outputs:

- Persona messaging matrix
- Objection handling guide
- Persona-specific assets
- Sales talk tracks
- Email sequences

KPIs:

- Persona assets completed
- Sales usage of assets
- Persona engagement rate
- Objection coverage
- Meeting conversion by persona

RACI:

- Responsible: Marketing
- Accountable: Marketing Head
- Consulted: Sales, Solution Consultants, Sustainability/ESG SMEs
- Informed: Director Marketing

AI agents used:

- Persona messaging agent
- Objection handling agent
- Email sequence agent

Templates:

- Persona messaging matrix
- Objection handling guide
- Email sequence template

Review frequency:

- Biweekly

Optimization triggers:

- Persona engagement is low.
- Sales reports repeated objections.
- A new stakeholder enters deals.

## WF5: TOFU Demand Creation

Purpose:

Generate awareness and early demand for the solution.

Inputs:

- ICP
- Target account list
- Domain playbook
- Persona messaging matrix
- Campaign budget
- Channel plan

Activities:

- Publish thought leadership.
- Run PR campaigns.
- Run LinkedIn campaigns.
- Host events, podcasts, and webinars.
- Activate analyst relations where relevant.
- Retarget engaged accounts.

Outputs:

- TOFU campaign plan
- Thought leadership assets
- Webinar/event plan
- Engaged account list
- Early-stage leads

KPIs:

- Reach in target accounts
- Engaged accounts
- Content engagement
- Webinar registrations
- MQLs sourced
- Cost per engaged account

RACI:

- Responsible: Marketing
- Accountable: Demand Generation Lead
- Consulted: Sales, Communications, Sustainability/ESG SMEs
- Informed: Director Marketing

AI agents used:

- Campaign planner
- Content drafting agent
- LinkedIn variant generator

Templates:

- TOFU campaign brief
- Webinar brief
- Thought leadership brief

Review frequency:

- Weekly

Optimization triggers:

- Engagement is outside ICP.
- Content gets reach but no conversion.
- Cost per engaged account rises.

## WF6: MOFU Evaluation Activation

Purpose:

Move interested accounts from awareness into structured evaluation.

Inputs:

- Engaged account list
- Persona engagement data
- Account pain points
- Assessment or audit offer
- Workshop format
- Solution consultant availability

Activities:

- Offer sustainability assessments.
- Run DRS readiness audits where relevant.
- Conduct consultations.
- Host workshops.
- Deliver demonstrations.
- Develop pilot proposals.

Outputs:

- Assessment requests
- Workshop attendees
- Consultation notes
- Pilot proposal briefs
- Sales-qualified evaluation accounts

KPIs:

- Assessment requests
- Workshop attendance
- MQL to SQL conversion
- Consultation to opportunity conversion
- Evaluation stage conversion

RACI:

- Responsible: Marketing
- Accountable: Marketing Head
- Consulted: Sales, Solution Consultants
- Informed: Director Marketing, Sales Head

AI agents used:

- Assessment brief generator
- Workshop agenda assistant
- Consultation summary agent

Templates:

- Assessment offer
- Workshop agenda
- Consultation note

Review frequency:

- Weekly

Optimization triggers:

- Engaged accounts do not request evaluation.
- Workshops do not create sales opportunities.
- Solution consultant bandwidth blocks conversion.

RACI:

- Responsible: Sales Enablement
- Accountable: Sales Head
- Consulted: Marketing, Solution Consultants, Finance, Legal
- Informed: Director Marketing

AI agents used:

- Proposal drafting assistant
- ROI calculator assistant
- Procurement response assistant

Templates:

- Proposal template
- ROI calculator
- Procurement response pack

## WF8: Funnel Velocity Optimization

Purpose:

Reduce the time spent between funnel stages.

Inputs:

- Lead to MQL days
- MQL to SQL days
- SQL to Opportunity days
- Opportunity to Closure days
- Stage conversion rates
- Rejection reasons
- Deal blocker notes

Activities:

- Identify stage delays.
- Improve nurture journeys.
- Improve proposal turnaround.
- Assign solution consultants.
- Activate executive sponsorship.
- Use customer references.
- Improve AI-assisted qualification.

Outputs:

- Funnel velocity report
- Bottleneck log
- Stage improvement actions
- Experiment plan

KPIs:

- Lead to MQL days
- MQL to SQL days
- SQL to Opportunity days
- Opportunity to Closure days
- Stage conversion rate
- Bottleneck resolution rate

RACI:

- Responsible: Revenue Operations
- Accountable: Revenue Operations Lead
- Consulted: Marketing, Sales, Solution Consultants
- Informed: Director Marketing, Sales Head

AI agents used:

- Funnel analyst
- Rejection reason classifier
- Experiment planner

Templates:

- Funnel velocity dashboard
- Bottleneck log
- Experiment brief

Review frequency:

- Biweekly

Optimization triggers:

- Any stage delay increases for two cycles.
- MQL rejection rises.
- Opportunity aging increases.

## WF9: MQL to SQL to Closure Engine

Purpose:

Operationalize lead qualification, sales handoff, opportunity creation, and closure discipline.

Inputs:

- Lead source
- Engagement behavior
- Account fit score
- Buying intent
- Persona role
- Sales feedback
- Opportunity stage data

Activities:

- Define MQL criteria.
- Define SQL criteria.
- Score leads.
- Route leads to Sales.
- Track handoff SLA.
- Track conversion to opportunity and win.
- Review rejection reasons.

Outputs:

- MQL definition
- SQL definition
- Lead scoring model
- Handoff SLA
- Conversion dashboard
- Rejection reason report

KPIs:

- MQLs generated
- MQL to SQL conversion
- SQL to Opportunity conversion
- Opportunity to Win Rate
- Average Deal Size
- Pipeline Coverage
- Handoff SLA adherence

RACI:

- Responsible: Sales + Marketing
- Accountable: Sales Head + Marketing Head
- Consulted: Revenue Operations, Solution Consultants
- Informed: Director Marketing

AI agents used:

- Lead scoring assistant
- Handoff summary agent
- Rejection reason classifier

Templates:

- MQL/SQL definition
- Lead scoring model
- Handoff SLA

Review frequency:

- Weekly

Optimization triggers:

- Sales rejects MQLs.
- SQL conversion falls.
- Pipeline coverage is below target.

## WF10: ROI And Proof Loop

Purpose:

Measure commercial and sustainability outcomes, then turn them into proof for future demand and conversion.

Inputs:

- Campaign performance
- Revenue sourced
- Revenue influenced
- Closed-won data
- Delivery outcomes
- Sustainability impact metrics
- Customer feedback

Activities:

- Track CAC, cost per MQL, cost per SQL, campaign ROI.
- Track revenue sourced and influenced.
- Track payback period.
- Track compliance achieved, assets liquidated, tonnes diverted, carbon emissions avoided, containers returned, deposit redemption rate where relevant.
- Build case studies and testimonials.
- Feed proof into campaigns and sales enablement.

Outputs:

- ROI dashboard
- Sustainability impact report
- Case study
- Testimonial
- Reference account list
- Updated campaign and sales assets

KPIs:

- Campaign ROI
- Revenue sourced
- Revenue influenced
- Payback period
- Case studies completed
- Reference accounts available
- Sustainability outcomes proven

RACI:

- Responsible: Marketing
- Accountable: Marketing Head
- Consulted: Sales, Customer Success, Finance, Sustainability/ESG SMEs
- Informed: Director Marketing, Leadership

AI agents used:

- ROI analyst
- Case study writer
- Impact report assistant

Templates:

- ROI dashboard
- Case study template
- Impact proof brief

Review frequency:

- Monthly

Optimization triggers:

- Campaign ROI is unclear.
- Sales lacks proof for objections.
- Customer outcomes are delivered but not converted into assets.
