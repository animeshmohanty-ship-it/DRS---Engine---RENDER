# SMS RACI Matrix

Version: 0.1

## Role Definitions

- Director Marketing: owns SMS architecture, system health, and capability building.
- Marketing Head: owns domain authority, messaging, campaigns, and proof assets.
- Demand Generation Lead: owns TOFU demand creation and campaign performance.
- Sales Head: owns sales acceptance, opportunity conversion, and closure.
- Revenue Operations Lead: owns funnel measurement, handoff discipline, and velocity.
- Solution Consultants: own solution-specific evaluation, workshops, assessments, and proposal inputs.
- Sustainability/ESG SMEs: validate domain, regulation, and impact claims.
- Finance: supports ROI, commercial models, CAC, payback, and proposal economics.
- Customer Success: supports outcome proof, case studies, and references.

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to Director Marketing when:

- Solution positioning is unclear or inconsistent.
- Marketing and Sales disagree on ICP, MQL, or SQL definitions.
- A solution requires new system-level assets or capabilities.
- Impact or regulatory claims create credibility risk.

Escalate to Sales Head when:

- MQL to SQL conversion falls below target.
- Opportunities are stuck in proposal, procurement, or executive approval.
- Buying committee gaps block progression.

Escalate to Marketing Head when:

- Campaigns generate low-fit leads.
- Persona messaging does not match sales conversations.
- Proof assets are missing for repeated objections.

Escalate to Revenue Operations Lead when:

- Funnel stage delays rise for two consecutive cycles.
- Handoff SLA is not followed.
- Pipeline reporting is inconsistent.
