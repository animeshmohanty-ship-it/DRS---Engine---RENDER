# SMS System Architecture

Version: 0.1

## System Objective

Enable Recykal to repeatedly create, qualify, accelerate, and close enterprise sustainability opportunities across solution lines without rebuilding GTM from scratch for every offering.

## Strategic Positioning

Recykal should position enterprise sustainability solutions as:

> A single operating partner for compliance, circularity, and measurable sustainability outcomes.

This positioning connects regulatory obligation, business value, and environmental proof. It prevents each solution from being sold as a disconnected service.

## Operating Layers

### Layer 1: Domain Authority

Purpose: become credible before asking for demand.

Core modules:

- Regulation intelligence
- Competitor and category analysis
- Sector pain point research
- Benchmark reports
- Industry playbooks
- Use-case libraries

Primary owner: Marketing with Sustainability/ESG SMEs

### Layer 2: Account Intelligence

Purpose: focus effort on the right companies and buying committees.

Core modules:

- TAM/SAM/SOM estimates
- ICP definitions
- Target account list
- Obligation and maturity scoring
- Buying committee maps
- Account intelligence sheets

Primary owners: Marketing and Sales

### Layer 3: Persona-Led Demand

Purpose: create message-market fit for each decision maker.

Core modules:

- Persona messaging
- TOFU thought leadership
- MOFU workshops and assessments
- BOFU commercial proof
- Nurture journeys
- Sales enablement

Primary owners: Marketing, Sales, Solution Consultants

### Layer 4: Revenue Conversion

Purpose: turn demand into qualified pipeline, opportunities, and wins.

Core modules:

- MQL definitions
- SQL definitions
- Lead scoring
- Sales handoff
- Proposal support
- ROI analysis
- Procurement support
- Executive sponsorship

Primary owners: Sales and Revenue Operations

### Layer 5: ROI And Proof

Purpose: prove commercial and sustainability outcomes, then use them to improve the funnel.

Core modules:

- Campaign ROI
- Revenue sourced and influenced
- Compliance achieved
- Assets liquidated
- Tonnes diverted from landfill
- Carbon emissions avoided
- Case studies
- Reference calls

Primary owners: Marketing, Sales, Customer Success, Sustainability/ESG SMEs

## SMS Revenue Flywheel

```text
Domain Intelligence
  -> Target Account Selection
  -> Buying Committee Mapping
  -> Persona-Led Communication
  -> TOFU Demand Creation
  -> MOFU Evaluation Activation
  -> BOFU Conversion Enablement
  -> Funnel Velocity Optimization
  -> MQL to SQL to Closure
  -> ROI And Proof
  -> Stronger Domain Intelligence
```

## System Logic

SMS grows when each workflow strengthens the next workflow:

- Domain intelligence creates credibility and focus.
- Target account selection directs effort toward high-fit companies.
- Buying committee mapping prevents single-threaded selling.
- Persona-led communication makes each stakeholder feel understood.
- TOFU creates awareness and demand.
- MOFU turns curiosity into evaluation.
- BOFU helps Sales convert opportunities.
- Funnel velocity optimization reduces stage delays.
- MQL to SQL to closure creates operating discipline.
- ROI and proof feed case studies, benchmarks, references, and better campaigns.

## Decision Points

| Decision Point | Owner | Trigger | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| Select solution focus | Director Marketing + Sales Head | Solution priority or revenue opportunity | Solution instantiation brief |
| Approve ICP | Marketing + Sales | Domain and account research complete | ICP and target account list |
| Activate campaign | Marketing Head | Messaging and assets ready | TOFU/MOFU campaign plan |
| Accept MQL definition | Sales + Marketing | Lead scoring proposed | Handoff SLA |
| Move account to SQL | Sales | Fit and buying intent confirmed | Sales-qualified opportunity |
| Create proposal | Sales + Solution Consultant | Problem, scope, and buying committee validated | Commercial proposal |
| Trigger executive sponsorship | Sales Head | High-value or stuck opportunity | Executive action plan |
| Build proof asset | Marketing + Customer Success | Outcome achieved | Case study, reference, or ROI proof |

## Feedback Loops

Weekly loops:

- Campaign performance updates account targeting.
- Sales objections update persona messaging.
- MQL rejection reasons update lead scoring.
- Proposal blockers update BOFU assets.

Monthly loops:

- Funnel velocity review identifies stage delays.
- Pipeline review updates solution focus.
- ROI review updates budget allocation.
- Proof review updates case studies and references.

Quarterly loops:

- ICP refresh.
- Sector playbook refresh.
- Solution portfolio prioritization.
- Enterprise revenue engine health review.

## Solution Instantiation Parameters

Each solution should be launched by filling the solution instantiation template:

- Solution
- Target sectors
- Regulatory or business trigger
- ICP
- Target account list
- Buying committee
- Persona messages
- TOFU/MOFU/BOFU journeys
- MQL/SQL criteria
- Sales assets
- ROI metrics
- Proof assets needed
- 30-day launch goals

## Minimum Viable SMS For A Solution

A solution can be considered ready for initial SMS launch when these exist:

- One domain playbook
- One ICP definition
- One target account list
- One buying committee map
- One persona messaging matrix
- One TOFU campaign asset
- One MOFU assessment or workshop offer
- One BOFU proposal or ROI template
- One MQL/SQL definition
- One weekly dashboard

## System Health Indicators

Healthy SMS:

- Target accounts match the ICP.
- MQLs are accepted by Sales.
- Buying committees are mapped before proposal stage.
- Persona messaging reflects real stakeholder pain.
- TOFU creates awareness, MOFU creates evaluation, and BOFU supports closure.
- Funnel stage delays are visible and actively reduced.
- ROI proof turns into stronger campaigns and sales enablement.

Unhealthy SMS:

- Campaigns generate leads that Sales rejects.
- Messaging is generic across personas.
- Opportunities are single-threaded.
- Proposals stall in procurement without support.
- ROI proof is not captured after delivery.
- Marketing measures activity but not revenue movement.
