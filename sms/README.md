# Sustainability Solutions Marketing System (SMS)

Version: 0.1

Owner: Director Marketing

System: RMOS / Project MARS

## Purpose

SMS is the reusable marketing operating architecture for scaling Recykal's enterprise sustainability solutions across sectors, accounts, and offerings.

It turns sustainability marketing into a repeatable revenue engine: understand the domain, identify the right companies, map buying committees, build persona-led communication, create TOFU/MOFU/BOFU journeys, accelerate MQL to SQL to closure, and prove ROI.

## Solution Portfolio

- EPR Compliance Services: Plastic EPR, E-waste EPR, Battery EPR, Tyre EPR
- Electronics Asset Liquidation: laptops, servers, networking equipment, mobile devices
- CSR Project Execution: collection drives, circular economy awareness, waste infrastructure, community engagement
- Alternative Fuel Supply: RDF, biomass-derived fuels, industrial residue streams
- DRS Readiness Consulting: regulatory, retail, operational, consumer, and technology readiness
- Plastic Credits
- Circularity Assessments
- ESG Advisory Services

## North Star

Enterprise Sustainability Revenue Index:

```text
(Qualified Pipeline x Funnel Velocity x Win Probability) / Customer Acquisition Cost
```

The system improves when qualified pipeline grows, deals move faster, win probability increases, and acquisition cost falls.
