# DMS RACI Matrix

Version: 0.1

## Role Definitions

- Director Marketing: owns DMS architecture, system health, and capability building.
- DMS Pod Lead: owns geography-level DMS execution and launch readiness.
- Marketing Head: owns evidence, narrative, consumer awareness, and amplification.
- Government Affairs Head: owns stakeholder mapping and alignment.
- Communications Head: owns reputation management, media monitoring, and rapid response.
- Operations Head: owns launch operational readiness and collection optimization.
- Retail/Partner Teams: own retail onboarding, site coordination, and partner activation.
- Data Team: supports evidence, collection dashboard, and optimization analysis.
- Leadership: approves geography priority and major launch decisions.

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to Director Marketing when:

- The core narrative is disputed or unclear.
- Stakeholder alignment blocks launch movement.
- Consumer communication may overpromise operational readiness.
- A geography needs system-level changes before launch.

Escalate to DMS Pod Lead when:

- Any T-minus launch milestone is delayed.
- Stakeholder, retail, machine, or awareness readiness is out of sync.
- Go/no-go confidence drops below agreed threshold.

Escalate to Communications Head when:

- Negative media or misinformation appears.
- A stakeholder concern becomes public.
- An operational issue risks reputation.

Escalate to Operations Head when:

- Return rate, active users, or retail participation falls below target.
- Machine, collection, or retail friction blocks adoption.
